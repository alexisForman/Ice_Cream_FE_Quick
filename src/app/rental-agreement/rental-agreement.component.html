Package Details

Booking

Renter must book 30 days in advance, provide a $100 deposit at booking and pay the remaining balance before the event. If booked less than 14 business days in advance, we will require the total price or your event package to be paid in full at the time of booking.

Cancellation/Reschedule Policy

If you cancel an event rental less than seven business days before your scheduled event date, your $100 deposit is forfeited and will not be returned to you. If you need to reschedule your event rental due to weather (ie. heavy snow or heavy rain), you will be charged a $50 ice cream restocking fee, and you will be able to reschedule your event to a new date and time. All requests to reschedule will be handled on a case by case basis. All rescheduled events will incur a $50 ice cream restocking fee.

Terms of Use

Renter is responsible for any damages that occur during time of rental. If damages occur, you forfeit your $100 deposit and it will not be returned to you. For extensive damages you will be charged two times your total package cost.

Self service users: The ice cream cart packages include dry ice which can cause injury to humans and animals if not handled correctly. You agree to have a designated server if you opt not to have us serve at your event. DO NOT TOUCH OR CONSUME DRY ICE! KEEP DRY ICE OUT OF CHILDREN'S REACH! You, the renter are liable for any damages or injuries caused from dry ice. Ice Cream Cart Co LLC is not responsible for any damages or injuries.

Pick Up and Drop Off

You must have a place prepared for the cart to be placed by the time of the scheduled drop off. Delivery is included in your package cost if your event is located in Camas, Washougal or Vancouver, WA, There is a $50 delivery fee for the surrounding areas. At pick up or drop off, the cart must be empty (with the exception of any remaining dry ice). If ice cream is left in the cart at pick up or drop off, you, the renter, will be charged a $50 ice cream restocking fee.
